Shopping List

The main purpose of this screen is to display all chosen products to you.

You will have the ability to "check" or "uncheck" an product, which will then move into the relevant list.

To "check" or "uncheck" an item, click on the TickBox located to the far left of the product item.

Costs and the amount of each product will also be displayed in an item.

The total number of products in each list is also displayed next to the heading

Product Search

In order to add products to your shopping list you must first search for the product you want.

When searching for a product, a list of Tesco specific products will appear.

Press the TickBox or click on the product in order to add it to your shopping list.

Individual Product Dialog

When pressing on a product item, a dialog will appear with information about the product.

Use this screen to add or remove the product from your shopping list, or choose the number of items you wish to purchase.

Tesco Store Screen

This screen will display the opening/ closing times of a Tesco store for each day of the week.

To configure which store you prefer to be displayed, press the "Edit" text next to the title.

Tesco Store Search

Search for a Tesco store near a location.

Enter a town, city or postcode to view Tesco stores nearby that area.

Press on a store and choose "Select" to make it your personal preference.
